How To Add To Home Screen

For Apple iPhone and iPad only

Apple requires you to save this app to your homescreen in order for our app to function correctly. To install this app on your phone, follow the directions below:

Step 1:

Locate the share button at the bottom of your screen. If you don't see it, tap on your screen to reveal a menu and look for a square button with an arrow pointing upward like this:

Step 2:

Scroll down a little ways until you see Add to home screen and select it.

Step 3:

The app will pre-fill the details. All you need to do here is tap on Add in the top right corner.

Step 4:

Now the app will be installed on your device. Locate it on your device and tap on the Kelly Farm Pool icon like in the image below

Step 5:

You will now be asked to verify your membership type. By default, both options are selected. Uncheck one of the boxes leaving the membership type you have selected. For example, I am a Class A member, so I unchecked the Class C option and kept Class A selected. Now click on Subscribe!

Step 6:

Your device will now ask you to confirm that you want to allow notifications. Tap on Allow

Step 7:

You are now subscribed to push notifications and you will receive a confirmation notification. After subscribing to push notifications, a option will appear to enter your phone number. Input your mobile phone number if you wish to recieve text messages from Kelly Farm Pool

Step 8:

You're done! Kelly Farm Pool will only send necessary notifications. If you ever want to opt out of notifications, you can open the app and look for the bell icon in the bottom right corner. Tapping here will allow you to subscribe/unsubscribe. To unsubscribe to text messages, you must respond to our text messages with stop.
